\section{Conclusion}

We presented a residual Kalman approach for short-horizon UAV bounding-box forecasting on FRED. The draft emphasizes that a tuned center-size Kalman filter is already a strong baseline, and that learned residuals should be evaluated against both this baseline and simple linear models over filtered state. We also introduced a decorrelation diagnostic that reduces acceleration predictability from position and velocity, with an optional term for global mean acceleration bias.
