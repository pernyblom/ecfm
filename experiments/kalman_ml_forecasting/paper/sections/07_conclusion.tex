\section{Conclusion}

We presented a residual Kalman approach for short-horizon UAV bounding-box forecasting on FRED. The method extends center-only Kalman trajectory forecasting to a full center-size box state, then learns acceleration residuals from event representations, box history, and filtered state features. We also introduced linear residual diagnostics and a decorrelation procedure that reduces acceleration predictability from position and velocity, with an optional term for global mean acceleration bias.
